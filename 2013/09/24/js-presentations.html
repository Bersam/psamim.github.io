JS/HTML5 Presentation Systems

  • (RevealJS)[https://github.com/hakimel/reveal.js]: very powerful, beautiful animations, markdown support, printable, controllable via socket server, speaker notes, multiplexing.
  • RevealJS slides can be created in (slide.es)[http://slid.es/].

  • RevealJS has a from-markdown generator at (reveal-md)[https://github.com/webpro/reveal-md]

  • (ImpressJS)[https://github.com/bartaz/impress.js/]: known for being fully 3D, impressive

  • ImpressJS has a from-markdown generator at (mdpress)[https://github.com/egonSchiele/mdpress]

  • mdpress has a Yeoman generator at (generator-mdpress)[https://github.com/btholt/generator-mdpress], with built-in features as timer for Ignite Show presentations.

  • (Bespoke)[https://github.com/markdalgleish/bespoke.js]: powerful, has plugins, simple and easy

  • Bespoke has a Yeoman generator for quick scaffolding at (Bespoke Generator)[https://github.com/markdalgleish/generator-bespoke]

  • (Shower)[https://github.com/shower/shower]: simple, easy, printable

  • (DeckJS)[https://github.com/imakewebthings/deck.js]: simple, easy
