JS/HTML5 Presentation Systems

  • RevealJS: very powerful, beautiful animations, markdown support, printable, controllable via socket server, speaker notes, multiplexing.
  • RevealJS slides can be created in slide.es .
  • RevealJS has a from-markdown generator at reveal-md
  • There is a docpad skeleton for Revealjs.

  • This is a remote controll for Revealjs.

  • ImpressJS : known for being fully 3D, impressive

  • ImpressJS has a from-markdown generator at mdpress

  • mdpress has a Yeoman generator at generator-mdpress , with built-in features as timer for Ignite Show presentations.

  • Here is a remote controll for Impressjs.

  • There is a docpad skeleton for Impressjs presentations.

  • Bespoke : powerful, has plugins, simple and easy

  • Bespoke has a Yeoman generator for quick scaffolding at Bespoke Generator

  • Shower : simple, easy, printable

  • DeckJS : simple, easy
