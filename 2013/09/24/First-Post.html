This is a personal wiki for me. Everyday I come across new tips, tricks, technics, websites or other interesting things which I want to have a place for them all. So I started this personal wiki.

I put it online so it would be accessible for me anywhere I am, and maybe I want to send public links to my friends.

To put it as simple as possible, I wanted all the notes to be in plain text and searchable with simple tools like grep or ack, to avoid the complexity of databases and servers or putting the notes deep inside the database and making it in accesible for everyday use. So I chose to write my notes in yaml format and publish them by jekyll.
